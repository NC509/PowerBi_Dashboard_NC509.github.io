# Dashboard
- This dashboard will be viewed from the following images (1-8). 


## Image Details
- Images 1 and 2 are from the "Global View" page where the locations of observed coral reefs in the data set are shown. The color shows the severity of the coral bleaching in that are over the average of time.
- Image 3 is the tutorial page for button navigation in the "Analysis" page.
- Image 4 shows a small information panel that explains what this dashboard portrays.
- Images 5 and 6 show the different lower visual views on change in temperature and bleaching levels over time.
- Image 7 is an example of where we can see what coral is at risk. In this example we look at the Atlantic Ocean, with coral at bleaching risk in 2015. In this region there was an average of 34.52% coral bleaching within that year. Of that region, during times of bleaching risk temperature conditions showed an average of 30.90 C°.
- Image 8 shows the global view of the region and timeframe from the example.
